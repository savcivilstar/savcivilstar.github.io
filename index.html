ABOUT

We introduce ourselves as Engineers & Contractors engaged in the field of construction of Irrigation projects, Drinking water projects, Dams, Structures, Building works and Telecom projects for the last 30 years.

We are registered with Government department in A-5 class capable to undertake works without limit of amount. We are equipped with modern machines and a dedicated and motivated team of engineers and skilled manpower. Timely completion of projects is one of our core strengths.

​

Initially Mr. Shyam Kanjani with Engineering background, in his individual capacity started taking up contracts of construction of Irrigation Dams/ channels/ Structures some time in year 2000 in the name of Shiva Constructions, and within a short spell of time got himself enlisted as

registered contractor in M.P. Irrigation Department (now known as Water Resources Department).

​

To strengthen the organization, it was decided

to join hand with people of experience in the field of engineering and construction and to change the constitution of the company for proprietary to a private limited company. The company was registered in the name of ShivaVikas Civilstar Pvt Ltd. The following persons from the field of engineering and construction joined hands as directors to take the organization to the new heights.

Mr. Girish Shukla, having vast experience in the field of water resources. He is a retired Executive Engineer from water resources department.

Mr. Vikas Dubey, owning a construction company in the name of Vikas Construction, having experience of construction of structures such as aqueduct, intake well, water treatment plant etc.

Mr. Manmohan Singh, having a experience of 30years in fileld of construction of Roads, dams and other infrastructure projects
